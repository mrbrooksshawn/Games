Pop Pop's Connect 4

  • Start as Player 1 and take your turn inside the box.
  • You cannot put your piece in a spot that is not at the bottom or does not have a piece below it.
  • If you take your turn and you did not win, it will be Player 2's turn.
  • The game will continue until a winning combination of 4 is reached by Player 1 or Player 2, or until the board is full and no winning combination was found.
